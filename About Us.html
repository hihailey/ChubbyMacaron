High Quality Macaron

Perfectly Crafted Macarons

An extremely beautiful Korean confection dating combination with a french traditional style,
our handmade macarons are baked in-house to perfection using an original recipe.
This bright and colourful chubby macarons are filled with various cream pilings, giving each macaron its own taste profile. They are a perfect complement to a delicious hot cup of coffee, or with a friend. You can also enjoy various character macarons with your eyes. Your eyes might be happier as much as your tongue.

Meet Our Chef

Meet Cindy, she crushed on Korean-Macarons. She has spent the majority of her life baking in Canada. Five years ago, she visited South Korea for her honeymoon, and she met totally different types of desserts.
There were various designs and thicker pilings of macarons, she really has enjoyed them. She wanted to bring them to Canada. After that time, she has dived in to studied her own recipe to make Korean-style Macarons. Cindy is our in-house macaron chef responsible for producing our fresh delicious desserts.
