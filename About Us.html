Meet Our Chef

Meet David, our scientist turned baker. With a PhD in microbiolgy and immunology, he has spent the majority of his life in research. From searching for a cure for cancer to finding a cure for your sweet tooth, David is our in-house macaron chef responsible for producing our fresh delicious cookies.
